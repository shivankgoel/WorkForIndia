What we do

We pick up various issues related to society and country, understand the problem, analyze them and try to dig out the root causes behind those issues. We also try to see various solutions that we can implement to remove those problems and motivate others to join us and further explore those issues and take action.The discussions varies from social problems , reforms and policy making.

Who we are

We are a group of students and professionals who are willing to think, discuss and analyze the problems of our country and share our findings with others. We believe that unless we figure out the reasons behind the problems we cannot work to remove them.We want the young and talented people like you to support and join us to work for the overall upliftment of country.

Our Aim

We want to inspire people to explore the root causes behind the problems. We want to share our views with them and motivate them to work for removing the little problems pertaining to our society and country.We aim to create a cooperative society where people think, analyze, discuss for the betterment of the country.
